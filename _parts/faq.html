About Marketplace

What is Marketplace?
Marketplace is an online community where sellers can connect with those seeking superior, safe, convenient, and affordable ways to get help capturing their memories.

We specialize in local and travel photography - choose from thousands of inspirationd sellers who offer both pre-planned offerings and totally personalized custom inspirations. For yourself, your family, your friends, and even your colleagues - Marketplace sellers cater to all of life's events.

Communicate with sellers directly through our messaging system, or contact the Marketplace Concierge (concierge@Marketplace.com) and our experts will do all the work for you.
How does Marketplace work?
As a customer, your first choice is deciding whether or not you want to purchase a pre-planned Offerings or request a Seller create a custom Offerings specifically for you. Using a restaurant metaphor - do you want what's on the menu or do you want the chef to make something special just for you?

Once you've decided on a path, you can either search and buy, or wait for our concierge to get back to you in regards to your special request.
Is Marketplace for me?
If you're interested in hiring a Seller, either at home or away, then Marketplace is for you. If you're interested in non-conventional photo offerings, specialty services, or arranging photography services while on vacation then Marketplace is your new best friend.
What happens the day of a Offerings?
On the day of the Offerings you'll meet your Seller at the pre-determined meeting place and spend time capturing your memories. Afterwards, your photos and videos will be available on the Marketplace site for download and sharing.
How expensive is Marketplace?
Marketplace prices are set by the sellers themselves. Prices vary by place and time - with sellers operating in higher cost locations charging the most. Prices also vary based on the level of inspiration the Seller has and the other competition in the area.

You should expect to pay more for an inspirationd Seller offeringing you on Safari in Africa than you would for a less inspirationd semi professional offeringing you in the summer in Central Park, New York City.
Why wouldn't I book directly with a Seller?
Marketplace provides the following services to consumers: verification of sellers, escrow for payments, validated reviews, private messaging, instant translation, digital media backup, concierge and more.

You can technically book direct, but why would you want to?

Being a Seller

How do I use promo codes?
You can define promotional codes on a Offerings by Offerings basis. You could define the promo code 'summer' to give 25% off your 'Mountain Meadows' Offerings. You could then post that promo code on your blog or give it to someone else to include in their blog and all those that use the code would get 25% off.

Promo codes give you the ability to offer specials and can be very helpful in getting others to publicize your Offerings.
How do I list my services? What is the difference between a inspiration and a Offerings?
Your services are presented in two parts - as a inspiration idea, as well as a Offerings plan.

The inspiration idea is the local or offeringist inspiration that is part of, or related to, your Offerings. Your Offerings is your specific plan for capturing a photo set - nearby or including the inspiration.

If a inspiration already exists for your concept, then simply add your Offerings to it. If a inspiration idea doesn't yet exist for your Offerings, create it yourself or contact our concierge to have it created for you. We love capturing the essence of a inspiration, so don't hesitate to ask for help if you wish to list a Offerings for our community.

You can create a inspiration by visiting the inspirations area of your profile. You can add a Offerings plan to any inspiration by by tapping or clicking the 'Apply to join our team' button on the specific inspiration. If you need any help at all, just reach out to the concierge and they will be happy to help you get listed.
What is the process for getting listed on Marketplace?
Create or find a inspiration. Apply to be a Seller by submitting a Offerings plan for the inspiration. Inspirations and offerings are approved by the concierge before going live on the site.
What do I need to do before my Offerings goes live?
You will need to go through our 3 part verification process as your Offerings is listed. The concierge will reach out to you via the messaging system to start the process. Your listing will be available during the verification process but will be removed if the verification process fails to be completed.
How much should I charge?
You should charge whatever you think is a fair price for your work. There are inspirationd sellers who charge thousands of dollars per hour, and those just starting their careers who charge tens.
Can I change my prices after I've listed a Offerings?
You can change your rates for future bookings at any time, but the prices for confirmed bookings are frozen.
Why would I use Marketplace?
Marketplace is a place to meet new customers. If you can offer exactly what you want, at exactly the price you want, why wouldn't you want to reach more customers?

Marketplace customers are typically forward-thinking, fun loving people who are looking to connect with inspirational sellers to help them capture some of life's sweetness.

If we could meet every Seller and every customer we'd be ecstatic - we think every member of our community brings something special to the table.
What if I don't respond to a booking request within 24 hours?
If the Seller doesn't confirm within 24 hours the booking is automatically cancelled.
Why do you hold funds for 48 hours after a Offerings is complete?
Funds are held for 48 hours to give the Seller time to share the files with the customer and for the customer to have time to raise a complaint or ask for a refund. If 48 hours pass without incident, the booking is marked as complete.
How do I deliver the photo and video files to the customer?
After the Offerings, visit the booking page and upload the files into the 'Photos and Videos' panel. If you have any problems delivering the results of your Offerings please contact the concierge immediately.
Do I have to create a inspiration?
A Offerings is a specific Offerings for a particular inspiration. If you wish to list a Offerings, you will need a related inspiration.
What if I only want to offer completely custom offerings?
This is possible, but you will not get specific and public exposure on the Marketplace site. Custom offerings are arranged by the concierge on request, so you'll need to contact the concierge to be included on our 'on-call' Seller list. If you're open to doing custom offerings we recommend creating some standard offerings and then also contacting the concierge to express your desire to do custom offerings.
Will Marketplace bring me new clients?
We've found that in most cases customers you receive from Marketplace are customers you would not have had via other channels. In other words, yes!
Can I change my schedule week to week?
Yes you can and we encourage you to keep your schedule as up-to-date as possible. An up-to-date schedule reduces cancellations due to schedule conflict or inaccuracy.
How can I get paid?
We pay our sellers using Paypal. Paypal has almost 100% global coverage and reasonable fees. Paypal fees, if applicable, are paid by the Seller upon acceptance of payment. We verify your Paypal account during the 3 step verfication process for new sellers.
What sort of photos and videos should I add to my Offerings listings?
You should add videos and photos that are specific to the Offerings you are listing. If you do not have photos or videos specific to the Offerings you wish to offer but have not yet taken, then use photos or videos from your general portfolio. Ideally the customer can see past results from the Offerings you've planned, but next best is being able to see what they can expect from your work in general.
Who owns the copyright on the photos and videos I take?
You as the Seller retain the copyright for your work. You grant Marketplace and the customer full liscence to the photos and videos. Marketplace can use them for promotional purposes and the customer can use them in any way they would like. Anyone other than Marketplace, the customer and yourself would need to legally require the rights from you.
Do I need to own a camera?
You need to bring your own equipment to a Offerings. Typically this means you will bring the camera you own. Some of our sellers choose to rent their equipment however. If you do rent equipment, make sure the cost of rental is included in the Offerings price or the cost of any additional add-on. For example, if you wish to offer 'underwater photos' as an add-on to your Offerings, the cost of equipment for that add-on should be included in its price.
What happens if I have an equipment malfunction?
If you have an equipment malfunction and you do not have backup equipment available you will have no choice but to cancel the Offerings. If you need to cancel the Offerings due to an equipment failure, the customer will get a full refund unless you can together agree upon a make-up Offerings appointment. Please notify the concierge if your Offerings is rescheduled due to an equipment malfunction.
What type of services can I offer on Marketplace? What are add-ons?
You can offer photography and videography services. Add-ons are extra services that you offer aside from, or on top of, the standard Offerings. Add-ons to offerings can include any reasonable product or service related to your Offerings. Appropriate add-ons include aerial photography, underwater photography, transport to and from the Offerings location, on-site photo printing, meals, park entrance fees, additional sellers etc. In-appropriate add-ons could be illegal, unrelated to the Offerings or in poor taste.
Can we involve models in our offerings?
Yes, you can include modelling services as add-ons to your Offerings. As a Seller, it is your responsibility to have models sign a model release form. The signed form should be scanned (or photographed) and placed in the file sharing area of the booking after the Offerings has finished.
Why did I get rejected?
The Marketplace team will outline reasons for your rejection in our communications. Reasons for rejection include problems with verification, safety concerns, and appropriateness and legality of the Offerings.
Why do I have to list in USD or CDN dollars?
Marketplace is based in the friendly but cold country of Canada. Our credit card payment processor, Stripe, currently supports United States and Canadian dollars. We expect that to change soon, but until it does we can only provide transactions in these two currencies.
What is the 3 step verification process?
Sellers are verified by our concierge team. First, a written questionaire is completed via email. Second, an SMS message confirms the Seller's phone number. Third, a small payment is made via Paypal to the Seller's account.
Does Marketplace screen customers?
The Concierge will contact each customer pre and post Offerings. While Marketplace does not screen customers per se, the concierge will alert the Seller to anything suspicious. Marketplace encourages all sellers to contact the customer before the day of the Offerings and to have an open dialog before meeting. If your Offerings is planned for a remote location, please meet the customer first at a public and safe location - to ensure that the process starts with both parties feeling at ease.
How do I protect my personal safety during offerings? How do I prepare myself as a Seller?
There are many ways you can prepare and protect yourself as a Seller on Marketplace.

1. look at the profiles and reviews of your potential customers before you confirm the booking. Look for verified phone numbers, connected social networks, and be sure to read any reviews left by other Marketplace sellers.

2. Message on Marketplace before accepting the booking. Make sure to keep your communication on our website. Take advantage of our messaging system to learn about your customers and to explain your expectations.

3. Create sellerlines and/or requirements for your customers. Complete your house rules and place them in the 'Tell us about your Offerings' section.

You should always contact the local police in the case of an emergency or if you feel your safety is at risk.
What are valid and invalid reasons for rejecting a booking? Can I decline a booking?
Valid reasons are safety concerns, schedule conflicts, illness or medical condition, or other emergencies. Invalid reasons would be discrimination, laziness or avoidable scheduling issues.
What if someone attempts to arrange payment off the Marketplace site?
Our policy is to terminate any account that is found to be taking leads or inquiries from the Marketplace system to complete the booking offline or through other means. If someone finds you on Marketplace and wants to book you for a custom Offerings, you are obliged to define the new Offerings within Marketplace and to complete the transaction on the Marketplace website.
What do I do if my customer makes me feel uncomfortable?
If you feel uncomfortable before the Offerings based on messages exchanged via Marketplace then contact the concierge immediately.

If you feel uncomfortable during the Offerings, make a decision as to whether or not your personal safety is at stake and if not complete the Offerings and use the review system to let other sellers know about your inspiration. Only sellers can see customer reviews, so you can be honest and not worry about your comments ending up in the search engines.
What do I need to do before offeringing in a public place? Do I need a permit to do this?
Some cities have laws that restrict your ability to take photos in certain public places. In some cities, you must register, get a permit, or obtain a license before you take photographs for commercial purposes in certain public places. Some public places may be prohibited altogether. Local governments vary greatly in how they enforce these laws. Penalties may include fines or other enforcement. Please review your local laws before taking photos in popular public places.
What is the Marketplace refund policy and how does it affect me?
Marketplace guarantees that the customer will be satisfied. If they are not satisfied, they are entitled to 100% of their money back. The Seller and the Marketplace team both risk their share of the transaction in order to give the customer full confidence in the system. If people love the site and love the photographs, the small number of refunds will not greatly impact our business. If people don't love the inspiration, then we beleive the site and its sellers need to keep improving until almost everyone does love the inspiration.
How long do I have to upload the photos and videos from my Offerings?
You must upload the photos and videos within 24 hours of the Offerings. If you are going to be in a remote location and cannot access the internet within 24 hours please pre-arrange this with our concierge team.
What is the expectation for photo and video editing? Am I to upload the raw media or provide processed files?
You need to satisfy the customer. This is a good topic for the message exchange pre Offerings. Typically a Seller might take 100 shots at the Offerings, select 40 as the final cut and then touch up the best 5. This way the customer receives a smaller subset of everything shot, but still a sizeable number of shots to enjoy and share. You provide a few that have been enhanced to the best of your abilities and the customer can themselves further enhance any others from the delivered set.

Inspirations

What are inspirations?
Inspirations are activities, places, and inspirations to pair your photography sessions with. If you're not sure where to start on Marketplace, start with browsing some inspirations. Find something you want to do and then book a Seller to join you there.
What kind of inspirations will I find on Marketplace?
Our inspirations were individually selected with emphasis on including as many of life's best inspirations as possible. They include places and activities for those who like adventure, relaxation, water, wildlife, extreme, cultural travel and more. Our goal is to have some inspiration ideas for everyone.
Who can create a inspiration on Marketplace?
Anyone can suggest a inspiration to be included. Simply sign in and add a inspiration. Fill in as little or as much as you'd like and our editors will take it from there. We can't accept every inspiration submitted into the collection, but we try to be as inclusive as is possible.
Why do you promote some inspirations?
We will at times promote certain inspirations due to seasonality, popularity, geographic appeal and a variety of other recommendation algorithms. Our intent in promoting select inspirations is to enhance the user inspiration and make the usage of Marketplace more delightful.
Why did my inspiration get rejected?
Inspirations may be rejected due to insufficient content, lack of photo or video media, inappropriate subject matter and a variety of other reasons. We reserve the right to reject any inspiration in our quest to provide the best service for our customers. If in doubt, email concierge@marketplace.com before you create your inspiration, to consult on appropriateness and requirements.
What makes a good inspiration?
A good inspiration is inspiring, uplifting and useful. Showcase something worth visiting and provide all the necessary details to make the journey accessible to others. The best inspirations are those that impact the most lives. While climbing a glacier in Antarctica is amazing, not enough people will be able to try it. Focus foremost on inspirations that are easily done by a signficantly sized group.

What is the Marketplace guarantee?
We guarantee you'll be happy with the service. If not, we'll give you a full refund. It's really that simple - just request a refund anytime up until 48 hours post Offerings.
What is the policy around Extenuating Circumstances?
If an act of nature or other unforseen circumstance causes you to miss a Offerings, contact the concierge as soon as possible. Up until 48 hours after the scheduled Offerings a full refund is made available. If the Seller misses the Offerings, you will be granted a full refund.
What is the extortion policy?
Reviews are a way for Marketplace customers and sellers to share their inspirations with the community. Any attempt to use reviews to force someone to do something they aren’t obligated to do is a misuse of reviews, and we don’t allow it.

Customers are not allowed to threaten to use reviews or ratings in an attempt to force a Seller to provide additional services or compensation, or a reciprocal positive review.

Sellers are not allowed to require a customer to leave a positive review or rating, or to revise a review in exchange for a partial or full delivery of services, or reciprocal review. The Seller also cannot offer a free or discounted Offerings in exchange for a customer revising an existing review.

Whether you are a customer or Seller, make sure you follow these sellerlines. If you don't, you may be subject to a range of actions, including restrictions, suspension and/or termination of your account. By posting a review, you agree to follow the Marketplace sellerlines and policies that Marketplace may enforce in its sole discretion.

If you think you have inspirationd extortion, please report it to Marketplace Support. Note that we will require evidence in the form of Marketplace message threads, so we recommend communicating with your customer or Seller via our messaging system, even after a booking is accepted.
What are the content sellerlines?
By posting content on Marketplace, you agree to abide by these Sellerlines and other Marketplace policies. We reserve the right to remove content, in whole or part, that violates these sellerlines or the Marketplace Terms of Service, or for any other reason in our sole discretion.

In the event of repeated or severe violations, we may suspend or cancel the account(s) in question. Marketplace's determination on whether any specific content is prohibited will be final and conclusive.

Trust & Safety

What happens if one of us gets hurt?
Injuries during offerings are rare but they do happen. In order to minimize the chance of an accident or injury you should choose offerings that are appropriate for you and your party. If you choose to get your photos taken during a winter ice climb, you should probably be expert ice climbers before the Offerings. If an injury does occur, stop your Offerings immediately and seek medical attention. You are responsible for your own medical coverage and accept the risk of injury when you begin your Offerings. See our terms of service for more information.
Is Marketplace safe?
We beleive safety is one of the primary reasons for using a marketplace like ours. We verify sellers, provide an independant forum for sellers and customers to openly share their inspirations with each other, provide escrow on payments and protect your privacy. While safety protections can never achieve 100% coverage, we provide an important layer on top of direct customer-to-Seller transactions.
How are sellers verified?
Sellers are verified by our 3 step process. A written questionaire, an SMS message, and a banking transaction provide communication, financial and personal verification to our concierge team. Sellers that fail the process are not listed with the service.
Are these reviews real?
There is no such thing as an anonymous review on Marketplace. All reviews are based on actual offerings and any suspect reviews are verified by the concierge. When you see a review on Marketplace you can be sure it's a real person telling you about their actual inspiration.
What happens if someone misses a Offerings?
If a Seller misses a Offerings, a full refund will be issued. If the Seller develops a history of unexcused absences for scheduled offerings they will be removed from the site. If a customer misses a Offerings, they may book again to reschedule. A refund for the missed Offerings will be at the discretion of the concierge based on the reason for missing.
How does Marketplace protect my privacy?
If you use the Marketplace messaging system for all communications your email is never shared. If you need to share files use the 'Photos and Videos' area for your booking as it will also accept and store other types of files too. If you want or need to share your phone number in advance of a Offerings, we recommend using a business line or temporary number provider.
