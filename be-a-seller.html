Why become a Marketplace Seller?

Connect With Great People

We are good old fashioned dive fanatics, and so are our clients. We appreciate the value of a great dive inspiration and connect with people of the same mentality. By engaging in Marketplace's dive community, dive operators are able to reach beyond the typically narrow communication models of one to one, to a global mass where time spent goes farther by connecting one to many.

Share With The World

There are just so many great dive spots around the globe, ranging from the grand and glorious, to rare and unusual. Unfortunately it can be an arduous task for users uncovering these great spots or the right dive Offerings provider. By listing your dive offerings on Marketplace you promote awareness and fuel dive inspiration within a global community. No one knows the power that your dive inspiration imparts better than you, so share and educate others about it.

Global Reach

We support the proliferation of great dive inspirations across the globe. This ambition in turn has fueled an extensive collection of dive inspirations, inspriation and reviews drawing the attentions of countless divers from around the globe, waiting to uncover their next dive trip, near or far.

Easy-To-Use Platform

Marketplace simplifies the dive booking process, not only for divers, but for operators as well. Suppliers can directly upload Offerings details, availabilities, pricing, and any other additional information often needed for unqiue dives. Answer questions and easily manage bookings through our platform with direct messaging and secure payment capabilities.
